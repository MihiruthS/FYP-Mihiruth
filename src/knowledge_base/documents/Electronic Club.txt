Electronics Club(E-Club), now affectionately known as the Electronic Club is the official student association of the Department of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering, University of Moratuwa. Established over two decades ago, Electronic Club has grown from strength to strength over the years.  Many activities are carried out by the students of the club focusing on community service as well as professional development. Through these activities, the students learn to be responsible to others and they improve their soft skills and leadership skills.
There are 4 branches in electronic Club. Namely, Main Branch, PR Branch, SLRC Branch and SPARK Branch
